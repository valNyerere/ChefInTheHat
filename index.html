About

Welcome to The Chef In The Hat

The Chef In The Hat is a restaurant located in the hub of Nairobi, a serene place.We specialise in both local and Indian cuisine as will be seen in the menu. Since our humble beginning in 2016, we have been able to grow and establish a name in the county with top reviews. Our main aim is to have people have a good time as they eat good food. The ambience will have you relaxed and at peace.
